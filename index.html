About Me

Somehow my career has always been in the social services field with a little bit of technology integrated from utilizing technology in an assistive manner to an educational tool. I gained the skills and knowledge to begin my journey as a full stack developer through Dev League, an intensive software development boot camp. Dev League focused on a project based curriculum. After 800+ hours of grinding for development experience, I became a Dev League Legend.
