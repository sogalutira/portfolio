About Me

Growing up I loved playing video games, especially JRPGs. As I grew older I found an addiction for MMORPGs, especially World of Warcraft, and Final Fantasy XI and Final Fantasy XIV. In college I took the social services path, however my interest in technology sparked brighter from using technology as an educational tool with my clients. That led me to explore the field of software development and fuel a desire to help society through technology.

I gained the skills and knowledge to begin my journey as a full stack developer through Dev League, an intensive software development boot camp. Dev League focused on a project based curriculum. After 800+ hours of grinding for development experience, I became a Dev League Legend.
